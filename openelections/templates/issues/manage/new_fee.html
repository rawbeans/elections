Declaration of Intent: Special Fee

The process for declaring intent for special fees is multifaceted. Before you submit a Declaration of Intent, please be sure you understand the whole process, the petitioning requirements, and the types of information you must disclose to the Elections Commission and to the legislative bodies.

See the full petitions guide for more info, and contact the Elections Commission if you have any questions.

When you complete this form, your group will have a petition created. However, this petition will not be public until the Elections Commission has had a chance to review the materials you submit. You,the sponsor, will be able to view your petition and edit your public statements. The Elections Commission will review new petitions within 48 hours (72 hours on weekends.) We will inform you, the sponsor, by email when our review is complete.

If you wish to petition on paper, please submit paper petition materials to the Elections Commission so they can be approved. Remember: ALL petitions must be approved prior to being used!
