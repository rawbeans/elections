ASSU Spring Quarter General Election

The polls have closed. Voting ended at 11:59 PM on Friday, April 8, 2011.

Results will be announced on Saturday, April 9, at 5:00 PM at the CoHo. We welcome all members of the ASSU and anyone interested in the ASSU elections.

Please provide any and all feedback about the ASSU elections — the good, the bad, or the ugly — to the Elections Commission at elections@elections.stanford.edu.

If you have questions or have observed an act of voter fraud (such as a candidate or slate attempting to view your computer screen while you vote), please contact the ASSU Elections Commission.
